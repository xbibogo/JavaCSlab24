Studies show that many families are struggling with their household expenses. The following pie chart shows the percentages of income a typical family may spend in five different categories.

Some tips on managing your expenses

  • Make a budget and stick to it.
  • Save a little bit every week.
  • Don't buy things you don't need!
  • Don't waste the things you have.
